Why you need a hobby?

Hobbies allow you to discover interests outside of work. They allow you to be creative and try all sorts of new things. If you are bored with your old hobby, choosing another can make your creative juices flow again. Don't push yourself, but you have many options, even if your budget is limited. It helps to relieve stress by keeping you engaged in something you enjoy. Hobbies give you a way to take your mind off the stresses of everyday life. They let you relax and seek pleasure in activities that aren't associated with work, chores or other responsibilities. Hobbies help you become more patient.
